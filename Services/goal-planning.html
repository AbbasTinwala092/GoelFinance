Plan Your Goals

You can start your financial goal with SIP or lumpsum investment in selected mutual funds

Child’s Education

Children's education is a big responsibility in every parents' life. You can use this goal option to set the target amount and start investing with our selected funds in relevant categories to ensure you have enough corpus to take care of the education expenses.

Dream Home

Everyone wants a home of his/her own and to buy the home you need lumpsum amount. Using this goal, you can accumulate large sum to be able to either pay for full home purchase price or towards the first down payment.

Retire Rich

Once you reach retirement age you will not have income from working so you will need passive income which can take care of all your financial needs. Use this goal to calculate and accumulate enough funds in your account to retire happily.

Child's Marriage

Child’s marriage is a major milestone in the lives of the parents, and we can help you plan that goal and meet the responsibility with ease.

Car Purchase

Buying a car is a special and exciting goal for many. It is the most wanted material possession. When you start earning and saving, this becomes a dream for many. It is better to plan and invest according to that plan to get hands on your dream car, use our calculator to arrive on a plan that suits you.
